About Shoshana

Shoshana's Studio was founded by Shoshana Klein. This studio aims to create an all-in-one experience when looking for a small events photographer and a graphic designer. We aim to make your experience the best it can be so that you can enjoy your event without the stress.

Shoshana recently graduated from Binghamton University with a Bachelors in Arts and Design and is an expereinces photographer of seven years. She is currently working toward her Masters in Business Administration, which she will complete in the Spring of 2023.

Shoshana is originally from Bergenfield, New Jersey, and is currenlty located in Binghamton, NY. She is passionate about her work and looks to excel in everything that she does.
